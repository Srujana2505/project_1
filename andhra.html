Andhra Pradesh Tourism

Horsley hills

            Horsely hills is a perfect amalgamation of the charming, rustic quality of
            Andhra Pradesh and the luxurious setting of the hills. It is well-known for
            its breathtaking landscapes. Horsley Hills houses the largest Banyan Tree
            and the oldest Eucalyptus Tree.
            
            The major tourist places around Horsley hills are Lake Gangotri, various
            viewpoints, Gali Banda, Environmental Park, and the Horsley Hills
            Museum.
            
            Horsley Hills is also one of the few places to offer Zorbing in India.
