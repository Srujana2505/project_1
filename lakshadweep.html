Andrott Island

                Andrott island is one of the largest islands in Lakshadweep and is known 
                for the Saint Ubaidullah Tomb in the Jumaat mosque. You can spot octopuses 
                and other marine creatures on this island. Andrott Island is one of the best 
                places to visit in Lakshadweep if you are a history buff. Andrott island is
                also famous for the ancient Buddhist ruins found here.

Amini Beach: For Scuba Diving

                Amini Beach is an incredible spot for people who are looking for serene 
                tourist places in Lakshadweep. Located in Amini Island, this beach is popular 
                as it is covered with coral sandstone. 
                You can try various adventurous activities like snorkeling, scuba diving, 
                reef walking, and kayaking.
