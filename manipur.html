Ukhrul

                Being the highest hill station in Manipur, Ukhrul is mesmerising district 
                of Manipur that is a must visit and sure to grab the attention of the tourists. 
                The enchanting and the beautiful view of the Ukhrul town is far away from the 
                infuriating crowd, the cacophony of cars, the towering and congested buildings. 
                Tourists visiting the heaven-like town is sure to provide plenty of options for 
                a sightseeing tour including the Shiroi Peak or Shirui Kashung which is a place 
                popular for the Shirui Lily. All the more the district is famous for the beautiful 
                Shirui Lily, Lilium Mackliniae Sealy which are only supposed to be grown on the 
                peak of Shirui Kashung.

Thoubal

                Located amid the hills and hillocks, the Thoubal town of Manipur presents a 
                distinctive beauty which is considered to be perfect holiday destination for 
                every kind for traveller including those seeking peace amid the pleasant climate 
                and a variety of activities, for the adventure enthusiasts looking to satiate 
                their wanderlust, Thoubal is even ideal for trekking and hiking. Also, the town 
                is a good destination for those who are up for picnics with friends or family amid 
                the abundance in flora and green landscapes. A visit to Thoubal at least once in a 
                lifetime is sure to give a unique experience.
